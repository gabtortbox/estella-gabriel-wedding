Our Story

A few pages from the journey

Before We Knew

We first met in 2018, when we enrolled in the same course at NTU's Asian School of the Environment, studying Environmental Earth Systems Science.

Alongside Wanqi & Xavier, we became a close group of friends (s/o to babies!) where we spent the four years in university together — late nights in the computer lab working on GIS maps, camping at each other's hall doing our coding assignments, going on morning runs. Long before this became a love story, it was built on comfort, familiarity, and a quiet sense of steadiness.

NTU Babies Friends First

The Routes We Shared

As the years went on, our friendship grew through shared experiences and travel. Korea after graduation. Cebu for an outdoor adventure. Japan for snowboarding.

These journeys were not just holidays, but memories that deepened our closeness, shaped our friendship, and quietly became part of the story that led us here.

Korea Cebu Japan

Then It Became Us

What brought us closer was not one single moment, but a series of shared interests, choices, and experiences that slowly made the story feel different.

From a baking course to a barista course at Bettr Academy, and from Tioman to Taiwan to Okinawa — we kept finding joy in learning, exploring, and discovering new things side by side. Diving, especially, became part of that journey, bringing trust, calm, and a deeper closeness that stayed with us.

Tioman Taiwan Okinawa Diving

The Next Chapter

What began as friendship became partnership, and what grew through years of shared experiences, places, and quiet turns now brings us here.

This celebration is not only about a day, but about the life we have been building together — shaped by the people we love and the journey that brought us here. We are so happy to share this joy with you.

Good To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the RSVP deadline?
Please RSVP by 31 January 2027, about two months before the wedding. This helps us finalise numbers with the venue.
What should I wear?
The vibe is formal but funky, loud or pastel colours, depending on our florals (we'll confirm closer to the day). The venue can get cold, so we'd recommend bringing a jacket along. See the full details on the dress code section.
How do I get to the venue?
We've got maps and step-by-step instructions for car, taxi/PHV and MRT in the directions section above. There's also a QR code for free Sentosa entry, no need to message us separately.
When should I arrive?
Guests should be seated by 6:30 pm. You're welcome to arrive as early as 6 pm for cocktails and canapés, grab a drink and mingle with the rest of the guests.
What's on the menu?
We'll be having a 6-course Chinese dinner. If you have any dietary requirements, please note them in your RSVP.
Will there be drinks?
Yes! We're planning to serve red & white wines, gin, vodka and a few other options. You're also welcome to bring your own, corkage is waived (to be confirmed). If you have a favourite bottle you'd like to share with us, bring it along.
Are children invited?
Yes, we'd be honoured to have your family join us. Alcoholic beverages will be served, but this is a family-friendly event. If you're bringing kids, please let us know in advance (or note it in your RSVP form), and tell us whether you'd like us to cater food for them or if you'll bring your own for the little ones.
Can we park at the venue?
Valet drop-off at Oasia Resort Sentosa is complimentary, but covers only about 20% of our guaranteed attendance. Parking coupons are on a first come, first serve basis.

Self-park at Village Hotel Sentosa is a paid parking and a short walk to Oasia.
Can I bring a +1?
Yes! Please let us know in advance or indicate it on your RSVP form so we can plan seating and catering.
Where can I find nearby accommodation?
The most convenient hotels are right next door, Oasia Resort Sentosa (our venue) and Village Hotel Sentosa. Other nearby options on Sentosa include Shangri-La Rasa Sentosa and Capella Singapore, if you fancy making a weekend of it.
Still have a question? Drop us a message anytime.
