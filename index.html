Our Story

A few pages from the journey

Before We Knew

We first met in 2018, when we enrolled in the same course at NTU's Asian School of the Environment, studying Environmental Earth Systems Science.

What began as friendship grew quietly and naturally over time. Alongside two others from the same course, we became a close group of four — spending years studying, laughing, and moving through life together. Long before this became a love story, it was already built on comfort, familiarity, and a friendship that felt easy from the start.

NTU 2018 Friends First

The Routes We Shared

As the years went on, our friendship grew through shared experiences and travel. Korea after graduation. Cebu for an outdoor adventure. Japan for snowboarding.

These journeys were not just holidays, but memories that deepened our closeness, shaped our friendship, and quietly became part of the story that led us here.

Korea Cebu Japan

Then It Became Us

What brought us closer was not one single moment, but a series of shared interests, choices, and experiences that slowly made the story feel different.

From a baking course to a barista course at Bettr Academy, and from Tioman to Taiwan to Okinawa — we kept finding joy in learning, exploring, and discovering new things side by side. Diving, especially, became part of that journey, bringing trust, calm, and a deeper closeness that stayed with us.

Tioman Taiwan Okinawa Diving

The Next Chapter

What began as friendship became partnership, and what grew through years of shared experiences, places, and quiet turns now brings us here.

This celebration is not only about a day, but about the life we have been building together — shaped by the people we love and the journey that brought us here. We are so happy to share this page with you.

Drive into Sentosa via Sentosa Gateway. Use our QR code for complimentary island entry.

2

Follow signs to Oasia Hotel Sentosa. Drive up to the hotel entrance and hand your keys to the valet team.

3

Head inside and take the lift or escalator down. The wedding will be at the Aloes Ballroom.

🎟️ QR code covers Sentosa entry & valet parking. We'll send it to you closer to the date.

Self-Park at Village Hotel

1

Drive into Sentosa via Sentosa Gateway. Use our QR code for complimentary island entry.

2

Park at Village Hotel Sentosa car park. Standard parking charges apply — parking coupons are not provided for Village Hotel Sentosa.

3

Follow signs to Oasia Hotel Sentosa. It's a short walk from Village Hotel for the celebration.

4

Head inside and take the lift or escalator down. The wedding will be at the Aloes Ballroom.

🎟️ QR code covers Sentosa entry only. Village Hotel parking charges apply separately.
